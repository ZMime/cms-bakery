ABOUT US

Founded in 2010, ZMime Bakery started with a simple mission: to create delicious bread and pastries using only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods.

Our team of passionate bakers works tirelessly to ensure that every product that leaves our kitchen meets our high standards. We use organic flour, natural fermentation processes, and take no shortcuts in our baking.

What sets us apart is our obsession with quality and our commitment to giving our customers more of what they love. From our signature sourdough bread to our mouthwatering cookies, every bite is a testament to our dedication.
